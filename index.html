--- title: Call for the 25th Central European Number Theory Conference ---

The 25th Central European Number Theory Conference will be held August 28 - September 1, 2023 in Sopron, Hungary.

This conference is the successor of the traditional Czech and Slovak International Conference on Number Theory which has been organized since 1972, followed by the first (24th) CENT organized by J. Selye University, Komárno, Slovakia in 2019.

The suggested arrival day is August 27 (Sunday).

Registration is now closed.
Information: László Szalay, szalay.laszlo@uni-sopron.hu
